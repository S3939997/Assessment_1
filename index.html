Ideal Job

Network Administrator

Description of the job

A Network Administrator to responsible for the installation of hardware and software that is specific to the network of a company including servers and user management software. Network Administrators are always expected to maintain the network which includes managing user accounts and fault resolution.

Why does this job appeal to me?

This job appeals to me as Network Administrators are in control of every facet of a network, this is so if things go wrong they are able to diagnose and resolve issues. The diagnoses and problem solving aspect is what appeals to me the most.

What skills are required?

This job requires a high level of understand of firewalls, user management software such as Active Directory, network traffic control such as proxies and VPNs and the ability to navigate internal servers.

What skills I have?

Currently I have a high understand of the physical hardware required for a network and how to install the software. I also have a strong grasp of Azure Active Directory and end-user software such as Microsoft Exchange Server.

How would I obtain the skills I need?

I plan on learning this information via university and in person training at work or at University. If specific programs are required I will learn the information from online videos or articles.
